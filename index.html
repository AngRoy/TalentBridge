How it works in 3 steps

From widget click to AI-scored application, every step is traceable and explainable—ideal for technical judging panels and real deployments.

Step 1 · Engage
TalentBridge is triggered from the Zoho SalesIQ widget. Zobot collects role, skills and location, then calls a FindJobs plug, which queries the ATS and returns curated roles.
Step 2 · Apply
Candidate confirms a role, shares contact details and a resume link. A SendOTP plug runs phone verification; on success, CreateApplication writes into the ATS.
Step 3 · Score & track
The backend invokes an LLM to generate fit scores and summaries per job. Candidates can use “My applications” in chat to see statuses synced from the ATS in real time.
